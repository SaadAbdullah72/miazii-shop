Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 22, 2024

Welcome to Miazi Shop. Your privacy is critically important to us.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, place an order, or contact us. This includes:

  • Name and Email Address
  • Shipping Address
  • Phone Number

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders.
  • Send you order confirmations and updates via push notifications.
  • Improve our shop experience.

3. Data Protection

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you place an order or enter, submit, or access your personal information.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties except for the purpose of order fulfillment (e.g., shipping carriers and payment processors like SSLCommerz).

5. Push Notifications

Our app uses the Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) service to send order status updates. You can opt-out of these notifications at any time through your device settings.

6. User Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you and to ask for it to be corrected or deleted. Please contact us via the app for such requests.

7. Contact Us

If there are any questions regarding this privacy policy, you may contact us using the information on our website.
